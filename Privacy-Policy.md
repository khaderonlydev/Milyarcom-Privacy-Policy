# Privacy Policy for Milyarcom

**Last updated:** May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Ravado Tech** (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile game **Milyarcom** (“Game”, “App”, or “Service”).

Milyarcom is a simple and addictive bubble shooter game with cute farm animals, colorful bubbles, relaxing gameplay, and smooth controls.

By downloading, installing, or using Milyarcom, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Milyarcom does not require users to create an account and does not ask users to directly provide personal information such as name, address, phone number, or email address inside the Game.

However, some information may be collected automatically by the Game or by third-party services used inside the Game.

### 1.1 Information collected automatically

Depending on the version of the Game, device, region, and installed services, the following information may be collected automatically:

- Device information, such as device model, operating system version, language, country, and app version
- Gameplay information, such as level progress, game interactions, session activity, and basic performance data
- Diagnostic information, such as crash logs, error reports, loading issues, and technical performance data
- Advertising-related information, such as advertising ID, ad impressions, clicks, and ad performance data, if ads are shown in the Game
- Approximate location, such as country or region, usually inferred from IP address or device settings

We do not intentionally collect precise GPS location.

Information collected through the Game may be used for the following purposes:

- To operate and maintain the Game
- To save or improve gameplay experience
- To fix bugs, crashes, and technical issues
- To understand how users interact with the Game
- To improve levels, difficulty, performance, and features
- To show ads, if advertising is included in the Game

## 3. Advertising

Milyarcom may display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, rewarded ads, or other ad formats.

Advertising partners may collect and use certain information to provide, measure, and improve advertising. This may include:

- Advertising ID
- Device information
- App interactions
- Ad views and clicks
- Approximate location
- Diagnostic and performance data

Advertising partners may use this information to show contextual or personalized ads, depending on your device settings, consent choices, region, and applicable law.

You can limit ad personalization from your Android device settings:

**Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID**  
or  
**Settings > Privacy > Ads**

The exact menu path may vary depending on your Android version and device manufacturer.
